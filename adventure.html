White Water Rafting


White water rafting is taking a raft through white water.
White water is a form of river water that is characterized by fast, shallow stretches of water.

Near our Hotel Resort we have an area were you can have the experience of rafting. We have a professional white water rafting team that will guide you.


Hiking


Hiking is basically walking in nature on any path or on a specific trail. Hiking only requires the person to walk at a normal to a brisk pace in order to reduce weight as well as get the fresh air from nature. Mountain Climbing is a sport or hobby in which a person hikes or climbs a mountain.

We have a seperate trail that our visitors can take when going on a hike. To ensure the safety of others through this trail, our Hiking Team is more than ready to get onto their feet so that no accidents may not occur.


Indoor Activities


At our Hotel Lounge Area we allow families and people to gather around to play some board games or indoor games. Some of the games we have and provide are; Chess, Scrabble, UNO, Table Tennis and Puzzle Games.

We like to keep our guests entertained throughout their stay at our hotel.
